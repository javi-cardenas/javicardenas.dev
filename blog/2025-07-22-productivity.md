# The Para Method by Tiago Forte

"Your mind is for having ideas, not holding them." - David Allen

## Overview

**Overall Idea:** Organize your notes for action.

1. Projects (Most Actionable)
2. Areas
3. Resources
4. Archives (Least Actionable)

**Projects:** Short-term efforts in your work or life that you're working on now
**Areas:** Long-term responsibilities you want to manage over time
**Resources:** Topics or interests that may be useful in the future
**Archives:** Inactive items from the other three categories

[Examples](buildingasecondbrain.com/para/examples)

## My Customization
### Inbox
In the midst of a hectic week, there usually isn't time to perfectly capture, title, and file away new items as they arrive. I like to have an inbox of daily notes. At the end of the week (Sunday at 8 am for me), I recategorize the notes in my inbox and update my active projects.
### Number the folders
Numbers keep the folders organized correctly from most actionable to least actionable.
### Hot and Cold
90% of the time I'm just interacting with Projects and Inbox so I added "Hot" and "Cold".
### Rules
Project and Area folders have an emoji and a README.md file. No more than 5 projects, in Personal and Professional respectively, are allowed at one time.
